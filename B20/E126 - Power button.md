\[E126\] Power button

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image2.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

Remove the cable tie.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image5.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

Loosen the screws of the valve next to the pump and remove the valve
from its original position in order to the easier assembling.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image6.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.16667in" />

Pull off the hose clamp from the fitting.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image12.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

Pull off the silicone tube.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image4.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

While you keep the indicated hose clamp tightly, you can pull off the
pump from the silicone tube by rotating the pump around its axis.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image7.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

Remove the hose clamp from the interior of the machine.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image8.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

This is the plug of the pump.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image15.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.16667in" />

Pull off and remove it from the cable hole.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image10.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

The pump is removable now.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image3.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

The plug of the button.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image1.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

Disconnect the plug of the button. Unscrew the two indicated screws.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image11.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

Now you can remove the button unit.

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image14.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:4.18056in" />

Replace the part.

Reassemble consists of the same steps in reverse.

Be careful with the silicone flange around the button while reassembling
the button!

<img src="./E126 - Power button//media/image9.jpg" style="width:6.26772in;height:3.38889in" alt="IMG_6298_k.jpg" />

Make sure that the pumps are in the specified angle. Otherwise the pumps
will not work properly. Use cable tie to fix the pump.
